Welcome to ASILS13

"Asian Symposium on Intense Laser Science (ASILS-13): Unraveling the Frontiers of Laser-Driven Phenomena"
Laser Plasma Research Facilities in India: TIFR Mumbai | IIT Hyderabad | TIFR Hyderabad | RRCAT,Indore .

Objectives of ASILS13

Thirteenth in the series of the highly successful series of symposia bringing together Asian and world researchers in the exciting field of ultra-short, intense laser-matter interactions and applications.


Topics of the Symposium

  • High to Ultra-high Laser Intensity Science
  • Attosecond Science and High Harmonic Generation
  • Laser-Driven Particle Acceleration
  • Ultrafast Plasma Dynamics
  • Computational Plasma Physics
